Gojo Satoru

The Honoured one

"Throughout heaven and earth, I alone am the honoured one."

Unveiled the Limitless Cursed Technique in a High-Stakes Game of Tag: To make his training sessions more interesting, Gojo once turned a high-stakes mission into a game of tag with his students, using his Limitless technique to manipulate space and effortlessly dodge their attempts to tag him, all while buying ice cream from a nearby vendor without breaking a sweat.

Defeated a Special Grade Curse with a Blindfold On: In a display of both his incredible power and his playful arrogance, Gojo challenged a Special Grade Curse to a fight with his blindfold on, relying solely on his cursed energy perception. He defeated the curse without ever laying eyes on it, all the while narrating a play-by-play of his actions as if he were a commentator at a sports event.

Hosted a Jujutsu Sorcerer Talent Show: Always looking for ways to lighten the mood, Gojo organized a talent show for the Jujutsu Tech students and faculty, where he effortlessly won by simultaneously performing a comedy sketch, a magic trick involving his cursed techniques, and singing, showcasing his lesser-known talents.

Invented a New Dessert Using Cursed Energy: Combining his love for sweets with his cursed energy, Gojo once invented a new type of dessert that could only be eaten by those with cursed energy. It became an instant hit among the sorcerers, though he kept the recipe a closely guarded secret, claiming it was too powerful for the general public.

Played Pranks on Other Sorcerers with His Techniques: Known for his sense of humor, Gojo enjoyed using his abilities for elaborate pranks. He once used his cursed technique to make it seem like objects were floating around the Jujutsu High, leading to a hilarious day of confusion and surprise among students and faculty alike.

A Spontaneous Trip to the Beach During a Mission: On a mission that coincidentally took place near a scenic beach, Gojo decided it was the perfect opportunity for an impromptu beach day. After swiftly dealing with the curses, he teleported the entire team to the beach, demonstrating his immense power and his belief in the importance of balancing work with leisure.

Outsmarted a Curse with a Game of Rock-Paper-Scissors: Facing a particularly cunning curse that boasted about its intellect, Gojo challenged it to a high-stakes game of rock-paper-scissors. Using his charisma and a clever feint, he outsmarted the curse, winning the game and capturing it without resorting to violence, all while teaching a valuable lesson on strategy to his students who were watching.
